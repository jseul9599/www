  • Research on Cellulose Derivatives

    Development of water-soluble cellulose polymers
    LOTTE Fine Chemical is actively developing water-soluble cellulose polymers that are used across many areas of industry, including high-performance additives for construction, paint, and other areas (MECELLOSE®, HECELLOSE®). We will continue to engage in research and development to develop competitive products that exceed customer expectations, and will also develop technologies for the commercialization of new cellulose polymers.

  • Research on Food and Medicinal Materials

    Development of food and medicinal materials in various areas
    The food and medicinal materials research unit applied water-soluble cellulose polymers to develop pharmaceutical coating materials (AnyCoat-C, AnyCoat-P) and a food additive (AnyAddy®). It is developing various functional medicinal materials through R&D and technology support, and is also researching food-related technologies that can lead changes in food market trends, such as the focus on low fat and food safety.

  • Research on Specialty Chemicals

    Development of high added-value specialty chemicals
    The company is developing new, high added-value specialty chemicals based on its amine and chlor-alkali chemical technology platform. We are also looking to develop eco-friendly manufacturing process technologies, in an effort to realize our vision.
