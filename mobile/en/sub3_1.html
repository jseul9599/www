Safety Management Vision

"ZERO" environmental safety

LOTTE Fine Chemical establishes and implements sustainable development and production plans that respect people and the environment. We use field-centered environmental safety management to strive for “Zero” environmental safety accidents. This will help us to realize our vision of a workplace that is free of accidents, pollution, and illness. Moreover, we build and implement environmental and safety strategies based on the consensus among employees on the importance of environmental management.

Management Principle for HSE

  • 01 The company recognizes HSE as its top priority, and therefore considers environmental safety above all else throughout the entire process of product design, production, use, and disposal.
  • 02 All employees undergo regular HSE training so that they observe all laws, regulations, and other requirements. They also perform HSE activities that prevent environmental accidents.
  • 03 The company will build an advanced HSE management system, and will establish a Process Safety Management (PSM) system that ensures the efficient use of resources and energy, prevents environmental pollution and accidents, and maintains clean and safe work environment.
  • 04 The company will engage in dialogue with local residents, companies, and our customers and shareholders, so that we can fulfill our corporate social responsibilities through Responsible Care (RC) programs.

Air Management
Investments in air pollution prevention facilities (Selective Catalytic Reduction, Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction, desulfurization facilities, etc.) have maintained air pollution levels below our internal standards. In addition, a Tele-Monitoring System was installed at major outlets for the real-time monitoring of concentrations of pollutant emissions.
Water Quality Management
LOTTE Fine Chemical treats wastewater from each plant at our epichlorohydrin treatment facility, and then sends it to a municipal sewage treatment center. We set a wastewater reduction target for each major source of wastewater generation, and make improvements to processes that can reuse wastewater.
Management of Hazardous Chemicals
There is a comprehensive management system in place for hazardous chemicals, from their arrival at our sites through to disposal. Hazardous chemicals used in the workplace are strictly handled in production, purchase, and use. In addition, new chemicals can be used only after approval.
Reuse of Pollutants
Organic by-products are replacing chemicals (methanol) at sewage treatment plants, and waste acids generated in our industrial processes are reused as neutralizing chemicals.
